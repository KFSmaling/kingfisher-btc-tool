Strategic Direction 2024-2026

Mission: We help high net worth individuals protect and grow their wealth across generations.

Vision: To be the leading HNW insurer in Asia and the Middle East by 2026, recognised for exceptional service and innovative products.

Strategic themes:
1. Growth: expand into DIFC and Singapore FA channel — target 40% new business growth by 2026
2. Product: launch Whole Life product in Hong Kong by Q3 2024 — target top 3 market position
3. Operations: reduce manual processing by 30% through automation and AI by end 2025
4. Customer: achieve NPS of +40 across all hubs by 2026
5. People: build digital and data capability — hire 10 specialists by Q2 2024

Strategic choices:
- We focus on HNW and UHNW segments only — we do not serve retail or mass affluent
- We grow organically in Asia — no acquisitions in the next 2 years
- We invest in broker channel — we do not build a direct-to-consumer model

Key risks:
- Regulatory changes in Hong Kong
- Competition from established top-2 carriers
- Talent shortage in data and technology
